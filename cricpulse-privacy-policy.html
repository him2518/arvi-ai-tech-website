Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Arvi AI Tech ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the CricPulse mobile application ("App"). By using CricPulse you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or phone number (OTP), we receive your name, email address, and phone number from Firebase Authentication.
  • Guest / Anonymous Sessions: If you choose "Continue as Guest", Firebase assigns a temporary anonymous ID. No personal information is collected.
  • Match & Scoring Data: Scores, player names, team names, and match statistics you enter are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore for live broadcast overlays.
  • Live Stream Data: When you broadcast a match, video and audio are transmitted directly from your device to YouTube (or any RTMP endpoint you configure) via RTMP. We do not store or record your stream.
  • Device & Usage Information: We collect anonymised crash reports and performance data to improve stability. This may include device model, OS version, and app version.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the CricPulse scoring and live-streaming experience.
  • To authenticate your account and keep your match data synced across sessions.
  • To power the live scoring overlay shown during your YouTube broadcast.
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs using anonymised diagnostics.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google LLC) services — Authentication, Firestore, and Storage — which are protected by industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Match data you create is stored locally on your device first; cloud sync occurs only during live broadcast sessions.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Firebase / Google LLC — for authentication, database, and storage services.
  • Google AdMob — for in-app advertising.
  • YouTube / RTMP Providers — your live stream goes directly to the endpoint you configure; we do not intermediate or store stream content.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Camera — required to capture and broadcast the live cricket stream.
  • Microphone — required to include audio in the live broadcast.
  • Internet — required for Firebase sync, live streaming, and ads.
  • Read Media (Images/Video) — used to access media for overlay customisation.
  • Foreground Service — keeps the broadcast running while the app is in the foreground.

All permissions are requested only when needed and explained in-context within the App.

6. Children's Privacy

CricPulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: You can view and update your profile information within the App.
  • Delete Account: Use our Account Deletion Request page to permanently delete your account and all associated data.
  • Ad Personalisation: You can opt out of personalised ads through your device's ad settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).
  • Guest Mode: Use the App without creating an account; anonymous data is not linked to any identity.

8. Third-Party Services

CricPulse integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google LLC)
  • Google Sign-In & AdMob
  • YouTube (for live streaming)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

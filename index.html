[ ABOUT US ]

Pioneering Interactive Systems

Arvi AI Tech is a pioneering software engineering studio operating at the intersection of artificial intelligence and interactive entertainment. We do not just build applications; we architect intelligent systems designed to adapt, learn, and scale. Founded on the principle that the next generation of software must be fundamentally context-aware, our core focus spans two distinct but synergistic domains: bespoke game development and custom enterprise AI solutions.

In the gaming sector, we specialize in cross-platform development using Unity and Flutter, creating everything from hyper-casual mobile puzzle games to complex, AI-driven experiences. We believe that integrating machine learning models directly into game mechanics—such as procedural generation and adaptive opponent AI—is the key to unprecedented player retention. Simultaneously, our enterprise division architects robust Generative AI pipelines, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) agents, and computer vision systems for real-world business automation. We take raw, cutting-edge research and transform it into highly optimized, production-ready software.

The Future of RAG Agents in Enterprise

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is fundamentally shifting how enterprises handle unstructured data. By combining the conversational fluency of Large Language Models (LLMs) with a factual, continually updated vector database of internal company documents, RAG agents eliminate the "hallucination" problem that has historically plagued AI adoption in corporate environments.

Procedural Generation in Mobile Games using AI

Keeping players engaged in mobile puzzle games requires a constant stream of new, challenging content. Traditionally, level designers had to painstakingly craft every single maze and obstacle course by hand. Today, we are leveraging generative AI and machine learning to build procedural generation engines that construct infinite, unique levels on the fly.

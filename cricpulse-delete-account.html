CricPulse — Account & Data Deletion Request

What will be deleted

When you submit a deletion request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (Google / Phone / Guest)
  • All match data and scoring history linked to your account
  • Any profile information stored in our database
  • Broadcast session records associated with your account

What is retained

The following may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics (not linked to you)
  • Records required by applicable law for up to 90 days

How to request deletion

Send us an email from the address linked to your CricPulse account. We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation once complete.

Note: Please send the request from the email or phone number used to register your account so we can verify your identity before deleting data.
